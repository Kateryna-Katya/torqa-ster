Personal Data Processing Policy

Last updated: January 6, 2026

1. General Provisions

This Personal Data Processing Policy (hereinafter — "Policy") defines the procedure and conditions for processing personal data undertaken by Torqa Ster (hereinafter — "Operator"), and establishes measures to ensure the security of this data.

1.1. The Operator considers the observance of human and civil rights and freedoms when processing personal data, including the protection of rights to privacy, personal, and family secrets, as its most important goal and condition for carrying out its activities.

1.2. This Policy applies to all information that the Operator may receive about visitors (hereinafter — "Users") of the website torqa-ster.org.

2. Basic Concepts Used in the Policy

  • Website — a set of graphic and information materials, as well as computer programs and databases that ensure their availability on the Internet at the network address torqa-ster.org.
  • User — any visitor to the website.
  • Personal Data — any information relating directly or indirectly to a specific or identifiable User of the website.
  • Processing of Personal Data — any action (operation) or set of actions performed with or without the use of automation tools with personal data, including collection, recording, systematization, accumulation, storage, clarification (updating, modification), extraction, use, transfer, depersonalization, blocking, deletion, or destruction of personal data.

3. Data We Process

The Operator may process the following categories of data:

  • Personal data provided by the User:
    • Full name;
    • Email address;
    • Phone numbers.
  • Data collected automatically:
    • The site also collects and processes anonymized data about visitors (including "cookies") using Internet statistics services. This data includes IP address, browser type, access time, and addresses of requested pages.

4. Purposes of Personal Data Processing

  • Identification of the User to provide access to the site's services.
  • Establishing feedback with the User, including sending notifications, requests concerning the use of the site, and processing requests and applications from the User.
  • Conclusion and execution of contracts to which the User is a party.
  • Informing the User by sending emails (with their consent). The User can always opt-out of receiving informational messages by sending an email to the Operator at support@torqa-ster.org with the subject "Unsubscribe".
  • Improving the quality of the site, analyzing its use, and developing new services.

5. Legal Grounds for Data Processing

The Operator processes the User's personal data only on legal grounds:

  • In the presence of the User's voluntary consent to the processing of their data, expressed by filling out the relevant forms on the site.
  • Processing of anonymized User data is carried out if permitted in the User's browser settings (saving "cookies" is enabled).

6. Collection, Storage, and Security Procedure

  • The Operator ensures the safety of personal data and takes all possible legal, organizational, and technical measures to protect data from unauthorized access.
  • The User's personal data will never be transferred to third parties, except in cases related to the execution of current legislation.
  • The User can update their data or withdraw consent at any time by sending a notification to the Operator at support@torqa-ster.org.

7. Final Provisions

  • The User can obtain any clarifications on issues regarding the processing of their data by contacting the Operator via email at support@torqa-ster.org.
  • The current version of the Policy is always freely available on the Internet at the website torqa-ster.org.
