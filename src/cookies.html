Welcome to Torqa Ster! To improve your experience with our website, ensure its proper functioning, and analyze user activity, we use cookies and similar technologies. This policy explains in detail which technologies we use, for what purposes, and how you can manage them.

What are cookies?

A cookie is a small piece of data (text file) that a website saves on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit it. This allows the site to "remember" your actions and preferences (such as login, language, font size, and other parameters) over a certain period of time.

What types of cookies do we use?

We classify the cookies used on the Torqa Ster website into the following categories:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These cookies are critical for the website's functioning. Without them, the services you request (e.g., secure login to your account) cannot be provided.
  • Analytical and Performance Cookies: These cookies collect anonymous information about how visitors use our site. They help us understand which pages are most popular and how users interact with the content.
  • Functional Cookies: These allow the site to remember your choices (such as language settings or region) to provide a personalized experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: These are used to evaluate the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.

Why do we use cookies?

The main purposes of using cookies on the Torqa Ster website are:

  • Ensuring stable and secure platform operation.
  • Analyzing user behavior to optimize UX/UI.
  • Personalizing content for educational convenience.

Managing Cookies

You have full control over cookies. You can change your settings or withdraw your consent at any time through your browser settings (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc.). Please note: disabling strictly necessary cookies may lead to the incorrect functioning of some features of our site.
